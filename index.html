Group Buy and Riot Games

An E-commerce Analysis and Recommendation by Elias Bacarro

Make Themed Skins purchasable Together by players, in Groups of 5

If League of Legends emphasizes team play so much,
Why are themed team skins sold as a bundle to individuals instead of to groups?

Learn more

Offer a 20% discount for the total purchases

To encourage players to go through with purchasing as 5, we offer a discount incentive.

Learn more

Players buy more RP and
Have fun together playing themed team skins.
Everyone Wins.

Riot Games is compensated for the discount with additional unencapsulated sales and players get to seamlessly play team skins together.

Learn more

Perks

Implementing a Group Buy system would improve the League of Legends shop and overall player experience with both quantiative and qualitative benefits that fit in with the core philosophies of Riot Games .

Encourages Players to play as Riot intended - as a TEAM.

Everyone owning part of a skin set together would entice players to coordinate and queue up together instead of alone.

Learn more

Facilitates the Sale of Old and Less Purchased Skins

Wanting to be part of a skin set would lead players to buy a skin as a team even if they wouldn't buy it as an individual.

Learn more

Safe from Abuse

By restricting GroupBuys to Placed Ranked Teams, Riot Games not only prevents discount abuse but is also actively encouraging players to develop their friends lists.

Learn more

Encourages future development of Fun, Stylistic and Synergistic Team Bundles

The possibilities are endless for future skin bundles and sales! It would just make it easier for players to go from seeing something fun, exciting or cool to playing it out together as a team.
